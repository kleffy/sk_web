Welcome to the Southern Kaduna People Union Website

The Southern Kaduna People Union (SOKAPU) is a platform that brings together the people of Southern Kaduna, providing them with a collective voice and fostering unity, progress, and development. We are dedicated to promoting the rich cultural heritage, values, and traditions of the Southern Kaduna people.

About Southern Kaduna People in Diaspora (SOKAPDA)

The Southern Kaduna People in Diaspora (SOKAPDA) is an organization formed by individuals from the Southern Kaduna region who are living and working in various countries around the world. SOKAPDA aims to create a strong network of support and cooperation among its members, with the ultimate goal of contributing to the growth and development of Southern Kaduna.

SOKAPDA provides a platform for its members to connect, collaborate, and exchange ideas and resources, as well as to promote the well-being of the people of Southern Kaduna. The organization is actively involved in various community development initiatives, including education, healthcare, economic empowerment, and cultural preservation.

Through SOKAPDA's efforts, the Southern Kaduna diaspora has been able to make a significant impact on the lives of people in their home region, providing support and resources to help them overcome challenges and achieve their goals. By working together, the members of SOKAPDA are building a brighter future for Southern Kaduna and its people.
